# Article Rush - README

**Article Rush** is a browser-based quiz game for practicing articles across English, German, Spanish, Portuguese, Italian, and French.

## Key Features

**Core Gameplay**: Players choose the correct article for a gapped sentence before the timer runs out. Each round presents 7 sentences drawn from the selected language and CEFR level.

**CEFR Levels**: Six difficulty levels — A1 through C2 — let learners work at their own proficiency level, from beginner sentences to advanced grammar.

**Timer Modes**: Players can set a per-sentence time limit of 5 s, 10 s, 15 s, or unlimited, adjusting the pace and challenge to their preference.

**Star Rating**: Each completed round awards 1–3 stars based on accuracy, giving a quick visual summary of performance.

**Mistake Review**: After every round, players see a full review of all sentences with explanations for any incorrect answers.

**Grammar Reference**: A built-in modal covers article rules for each language — including English (a/an/the/∅), German case tables, and the definite, indefinite, and partitive systems of Spanish, Portuguese, Italian, and French.

## Tech Stack

Vanilla HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. No build step or dependencies — open `index.html` directly in a browser.
